About



Welcome to Little Lemon, where every dish tells a story of vibrant flavors, fresh ingredients, and Mediterranean warmth. Located in the heart of the city, we bring the essence of Mediterranean cuisine to your table, offering a dining experience that is as rich and diverse as the region itself. At Little Lemon, we believe in the power of food to bring people together. Our philosophy is simple: quality ingredients, expertly crafted dishes, and a warm, welcoming atmosphere. Whether you're here for a casual meal with friends or celebrating a special occasion with loved ones, we strive to create a space where you can unwind, savor, and connect.


Little Lemon was born from a passion for Mediterranean cuisine and a desire to share the flavors of this beautiful region with the world. Inspired by the sun-kissed shores, fragrant herbs, and wholesome ingredients of countries like Greece, Turkey, Italy, and Spain, we set out to create a restaurant that would capture the spirit of the Mediterranean. Our journey began with a simple vision: to offer fresh, seasonal dishes that are as authentic as they are delicious. From the moment you step into Little Lemon, you'll be transported to the Mediterranean through the aromas, colors, and tastes that fill the air. We take pride in sourcing the finest ingredients, from local produce to imported spices and olive oils, ensuring that every bite is a celebration of quality and flavor.

The heart of Little Lemon lies in our menu, which features a wide variety of Mediterranean dishes that showcase the richness and diversity of the region. From fresh, tangy salads to hearty stews, from grilled meats to indulgent desserts, our offerings are designed to satisfy every palate. Start your meal with our selection of Mediterranean starters, including classics like Greek salad, hummus, and falafel. For the main course, we offer an array of grilled meats, seafood, and vegetarian dishes, each prepared with care and attention to detail. Don't forget to leave room for dessert—our baklava, knafeh, and other sweet treats will transport you straight to the Mediterranean coast. Our menu is also designed with health and sustainability in mind. Mediterranean cuisine is known for its emphasis on fresh vegetables, lean proteins, and healthy fats like olive oil, making it a perfect choice for those seeking nutritious and flavorful options. We strive to create dishes that are not only delicious but also nourishing, ensuring that every meal at Little Lemon is a feel-good experience. Our Commitment to Quality

At Little Lemon, we are committed to providing an exceptional dining experience. We take great pride in the quality of our ingredients, the skill of our chefs, and the service we offer to our guests. Every dish is made from scratch, with an emphasis on traditional recipes and modern techniques. Our chefs work tirelessly to ensure that every plate that leaves our kitchen is nothing short of perfection. We also believe in creating an inviting atmosphere for our guests. Our restaurant is designed to feel like a home away from home, with warm, cozy interiors, friendly staff, and a welcoming ambiance. Whether you're enjoying a leisurely meal with friends or sharing a romantic dinner with a loved one, you'll always feel like part of the Little Lemon family. Join Us Today At Little Lemon, we're more than just a restaurant—we're a destination. Come and experience the flavors of the Mediterranean like never before. We look forward to welcoming you and sharing our love for food with you.
