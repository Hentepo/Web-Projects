Deserts

Indulge in our exquisite selection of Mediterranean desserts that offer the perfect sweet ending to your meal. From rich, honey-soaked pastries to delicate, creamy treats, our desserts are crafted with the finest ingredients, capturing the essence of Mediterranean sweetness. Whether you're craving something light and fruity or decadently rich, each dessert is a delightful experience that brings a touch of tradition and innovation to your table. Treat yourself to a sweet escape with our carefully curated dessert offerings!

  • Baklava - Sweet pastry made with layers of filo dough, honey, and walnuts, baked to perfection and drizzled with syrup.
  • Knafeh - A delicious pastry soaked in sweet syrup, made with thin noodle-like dough, and topped with a layer of creamy cheese.
  • Basbousa - Semolina cake soaked in sweet syrup, flavored with rose or orange blossom water, and topped with almonds.
  • Loukoumades - Fried dough balls drizzled with honey syrup and dusted with powdered sugar, often served with cinnamon.
  • Atayef - Small stuffed pancakes filled with sweet cheese, nuts, or cream, and drizzled with syrup.
  • Qatayef - A popular dessert during Ramadan, featuring stuffed pancakes filled with sweet cheese or nuts, then deep-fried or baked and soaked in syrup.
  • Rice Pudding - Creamy pudding made with rice, milk, sugar, and a hint of vanilla or rose water, often garnished with cinnamon or nuts.
  • Halva - A dense, sweet confection made from sesame tahini or semolina, often flavored with vanilla, chocolate, or nuts.
